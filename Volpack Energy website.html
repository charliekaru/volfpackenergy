Our Story

At Volfpack, we see two big problems: energy is too expensive, especially for those who can least afford it, and climate change is hitting harder every year. This isn't just numbers on a page for us; we've witnessed how these challenges impact real people and communities.

We've developed something special—supercapacitors that store more energy and charge faster than anything else available. While that may sound technical, what matters is this: we're creating energy storage solutions that work better and cost less.

We're also smart about how we operate. By manufacturing in Sri Lanka, we harness the exceptional talent in engineering, material science, and dexterity that our workforce offers, all while keeping costs down. We've partnered with some of the brightest minds in energy storage from around the world, because great ideas can come from anywhere, and the best solutions arise from collaboration.

Our vision is clear: to make energy storage accessible to everyone, everywhere. We believe that this is how we can have the greatest impact on both climate change and energy poverty.
